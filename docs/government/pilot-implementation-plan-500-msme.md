# Pilot Implementation Plan  
## Instagram Growth Copilot — Bhubaneswar–Cuttack Corridor (up to 500 MSMEs)

**Document type:** One-page operational annex · **Audience:** MSME Department, district administration, implementing partner  
**Posture:** Phased rollout with evidence; figures are **targets**, to be reported against actual enrolment with beneficiary consent.

---

### 1. Purpose and scope

The pilot will demonstrate **digitisation of Instagram marketing decisions** for micro and small enterprises—salons, cafés, retail, coaching, clinics, and neighbourhood services—within the **Bhubaneswar–Cuttack urban corridor**. The platform provides **decision support** (performance signals, suggested next steps, caption assistance); it does **not** guarantee reach, revenue, or replacement of human judgment.

### 2. Phased timeline (indicative — 12 months)

| Phase | Period (indicative) | Cohort size (target) | Focus |
|--------|---------------------|----------------------|--------|
| **Phase A — Foundation** | Months 1–2 | 30–50 MSMEs | Partner MoUs, trainer briefing, consent & data notices, baseline survey, technical dry runs |
| **Phase B — Scale-up** | Months 3–6 | 150–250 cumulative | Structured onboarding camps + helpline; monthly feedback; refine Odia/English support materials |
| **Phase C — Corridor expansion** | Months 7–12 | Up to **500** cumulative | Additional wards / Cuttack clusters; repeat onboarding; prepare scale learnings for policy |

*Dates may align with financial year or scheme cycle upon approval.*

### 3. Onboarding approach

- **Eligibility:** Registered / identifiable MSMEs and serious informal businesses willing to use Instagram for customer outreach; **explicit consent** for pilot participation and anonymised aggregate reporting.  
- **Channels:** (i) **On-site camps** with MSME associations / municipal wards / industrial estates; (ii) **referrals** through Startup Odisha / district industry centres; (iii) **helpline** (Odia + English) for account connection and first-week queries.  
- **Steps per business (target: under one assisted session):** account creation or login → connect Instagram (or mock/demo path where Meta approval is pending) → view analytics summary → receive **one** actionable “this week” suggestion → optional AI caption assist.  
- **Support model:** Tier-1 helpline; Tier-2 technical escalation; **no commitment** to 24×7 enterprise SLA during pilot.

### 4. Success metrics (honest, measurable)

| Metric | Definition | Reporting frequency |
|--------|------------|---------------------|
| **MSMEs onboarded** | Count with signed consent / registration record | Monthly |
| **Instagram accounts connected** | Technical proof of linkage | Monthly |
| **Weekly active usage** | Logins or meaningful sessions (defined in M&E framework) | Monthly |
| **Qualitative feedback** | Minimum **one** attributable quote per cohort (with permission) | Quarterly |
| **Support tickets resolved** | Time-to-first-response (internal) | Monthly |

**Explicit non-claims:** The pilot will **not** report invented DAU, revenue, or “AI ROI” without audited data.

### 5. Reporting structure

- **Implementing team → Project lead:** Weekly internal dashboard (enrolment, errors, support load).  
- **Project lead → MSME Department / nodal agency:** **Monthly one-page progress note** (numbers + 3 bullet risks + 3 bullet mitigations).  
- **Quarterly review:** Half-day session with nodal officers—demo, metrics, beneficiary voice (where consented), and **go / adjust / pause** recommendation.  
- **Data protection:** Aggregate reporting only in public documents; **no** publication of individual business credentials or private messages without consent.
