# Budget Utilisation Breakdown (Indicative)  
## Seed Grant: ₹25 Lakh — ₹50 Lakh  
### Instagram Growth Copilot — Odisha MSME Pilot

**Document type:** Financial annex · **Audience:** Grant committee, finance wing, implementing agency  
**Note:** Figures are **illustrative** within the stated band; final lines must match approved scheme heads and GFR / state rules. Amounts in **Indian Rupees (lakhs)**; **1 L = ₹10,00,000**.

---

### 1. Guiding principles

- **Pilot-first:** Funds prioritise **onboarding, localisation, and reliable operations** over speculative product expansion.  
- **Local value:** Preference for **Odisha-based** trainers, creatives, and vendors where quality permits.  
- **Transparency:** Quarterly utilisation statement against **this breakdown** (with vouchers / contracts as per audit trail).

### 2. Split at **₹25 Lakh** (lower bound)

| Head | Amount (₹ L) | Share (%) | Use |
|------|----------------|-----------|-----|
| **Team & delivery** | 9.0 | 36% | Product/engineering continuity (part-time/full-time mix), project management, M&E officer |
| **Localisation & content** | 4.0 | 16% | Odia UI copy, field guides, short video explainers, workshop decks |
| **Onboarding & field** | 7.5 | 30% | Camps, trainer honoraria, travel within corridor, helpline (SIM + modest stipend) |
| **Operations & infrastructure** | 3.5 | 14% | Cloud hosting, database, optional Redis/queues, domain, security tools, backups |
| **Contingency & audit** | 1.0 | 4% | Unforeseen compliance, minor hardware, external audit support |
| **Total** | **25.0** | **100%** | |

### 3. Split at **₹50 Lakh** (upper bound)

| Head | Amount (₹ L) | Share (%) | Use |
|------|----------------|-----------|-----|
| **Team & delivery** | 18.0 | 36% | Stronger engineering + dedicated pilot coordinator + part-time data analyst |
| **Localisation & content** | 8.0 | 16% | Deeper Odia materials, MSME sector variants (salon / café / retail), printable kits |
| **Onboarding & field** | 15.0 | 30% | More camps, Cuttack cluster expansion, peer champions, modest incentives for association partners (as per rules) |
| **Operations & infrastructure** | 7.0 | 14% | Higher-tier hosting, monitoring, optional AI API budget cap, disaster recovery drills |
| **Contingency & audit** | 2.0 | 4% | Legal/data-protection review, statutory variances |
| **Total** | **50.0** | **100%** | |

### 4. Exclusions (unless scheme explicitly allows)

- **Not** for land/building purchase.  
- **Not** for large paid media marketing blitzes.  
- **Not** for equity buy-backs or unrelated startups.  
- **AI inference costs** (if using external models) should sit under **Operations** with a **monthly cap** and **approval threshold**.

### 5. Reporting format (suggested)

Each quarter: **budget head → sanctioned → spent → committed → variance reason → next-quarter forecast** (one table, one narrative page).
