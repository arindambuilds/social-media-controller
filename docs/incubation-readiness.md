# Incubation & funding readiness (credible, minimal)

## Angle for Odisha / India programmes

- **Problem:** SMBs and creators rely on Instagram but lack analytics literacy and content support.  
- **Product:** Focused **Instagram copilot** — data + AI assistance, not a generic “all-in-one social suite.”  
- **Stage:** MVP / pilot — **evidence from real businesses**, not vanity metrics.

## Metrics to track now (honest)

| Metric | Why |
|--------|-----|
| Pilot businesses onboarded | Count with names + consent |
| Weekly active use (logins / sessions) | Shows stickiness |
| Instagram accounts connected | Technical proof |
| Posts synced | Data depth |
| Qualitative feedback | 3 quotes from owners (with permission) |

**Do not** invent DAU, revenue, or “500 users” without data.

## Pilot evidence to collect

- Before/after: **posts per week** or **stories per week** (self-reported is OK).  
- One **quote** per pilot (WhatsApp OK, then written consent).  
- Optional: screenshot of their IG Insights (with permission) — not required for MVP.

## Language that builds trust

- “Assist,” “suggest,” “based on your recent posts,” “pilot,” “feedback loop.”  

## Language to avoid

- “Fully automated growth,” “AI replaces your marketing team,” “guaranteed ROI,” unverifiable rankings.

## Startup Odisha / India / MSME fit (framing)

- **Digitisation of small business marketing decisions** (which post types to repeat).  
- **AI as assistant** to human judgment — aligns with responsible-AI narratives.  
- **Local pilot** in one city is a feature, not a weakness: depth before breadth.
